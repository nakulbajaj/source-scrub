Commitment to Privacy and Safety (Important)

  • This tool works completely client-side (meaning that your sensitive information never leaves your computer).
  • You are encouraged to temporarily disable your internet connection while using this tool for assurance that no information is sent via the internet.
  • All fields are optional, and should be filled based on whether the information was required while registering to vote.
  • Our source code can be found at github.com/nakulbajaj/source-scrub/ to further verify the security of this tool.
